Supplies Needed

(Updated Nov 29, 2024)

  • 1lb propane tanks
  • 20lb propane tanks (full or empty)
  • 8oz Butane
  • Backpacks
  • Bath Towels
  • Batteries (AA, AAA, D, C)
  • Body wash, shampoo & conditioner
  • Brooms
  • Camp showers
  • Camp Stoves/Sternos
  • Chainsaws (Gas)
  • Charge Blocks
  • Deep freezer
  • Diapers (newborn-toddler)
  • Dish soap
  • Dishwasher detergent
  • Extension cords (ideally 10 or 12 gague )
  • Fire detectors (batteries included)
  • Food (non-perishable, no MREs), particularly:
    • Beans (bulk, 50lb bags)
    • Cereal
    • Coffee
    • Cooking oil (32oz bottles or smaller)
    • Jelly (standard jars, assorted flavors)
    • Jerky
    • Maseca (bulk, 50lb bags)
    • Rice (bulk, 50lb bags)
    • Shelf-stable creamer
    • Shelf-stable milk (32oz tetra pak)
    • Snacks
    • Sugar (4lb bags)"
  • Generators
  • Gloves (for babies/toddlers/children)
  • Hand tools (hammers, etc.)
  • Hatchets
  • Household cleaning supplies (non-bleach sprays, wipes, etc.)
  • Indoor heaters (electric and/or propane/kerosene)
  • Laundry Detergent (Tide preferred, scented & unscented)
  • Lighters
  • Melatonin
  • Mops
  • Muscle rub/topical pain relief
  • Pet Foods and Supplies (litter, collars, pads, treats, ect..)
  • Pocket knives
  • Sleeping pads
  • Tents (4 person)
  • Underwear (new, packaged; adult, children)
  • Vitamin D
  • Wagons
  • Water (gallon jugs)
  • Wheel barrows
  • Winter hats
  • Winter scarves
