Your Donations Can Save Lives

Triangle Mutual Aid and Mutual Aid Disaster Relief (MADR) are actively providing relief to communities hit by Hurricane Helene. We urgently need your help to continue delivering critical supplies.

We are currently seeking large in-kind donations, such as:

  • Generators, power tools, chainsaws, and propane to restore power and assist in clean-up efforts
  • Shelving and storage containers for organizing and distributing supplies efficiently
  • Food, water, and hygiene supplies to help families survive
  • Emergency gear like space heaters, sleeping bags, and tents for warmth and shelter

Your support has already helped us deliver over 80,000 lbs of supplies to those affected by Hurricane Helene. With your help, we can continue to provide essential resources to those in need.
